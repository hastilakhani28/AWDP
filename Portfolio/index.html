About Me

I am Hasti Lakhani, a passionate Computer Science and Engineering student at Silver Oak University. My journey in technology is fueled by a fascination with Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, and guided by a core belief: that the most powerful innovations are those that serve humanity.

I see a world where complex legal systems can be made simple and understandable for everyone. As an empath, I am committed to using my skills in machine learning, data analysis, and software development to build tools that bridge the gap between legal services and the people who need them most. My goal is to not just write code, but to write code that empowers.
